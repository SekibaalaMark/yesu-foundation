Child Sponsorship

What Your Sponsorship Provides

When you sponsor a child through YESU FOUNDATION, your monthly contribution of $30 provides:

  • School fees and educational materials
  • Regular health check-ups and medical care
  • Nutritional support
  • Psychosocial support and counseling
  • Spiritual guidance and mentorship
  • Participation in social activities and camps
  • Career guidance and skills training

As a sponsor, you'll receive regular updates about your sponsored child's progress, including photos, letters, and reports on their development.
