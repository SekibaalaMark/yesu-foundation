Our Vision & Mission

Vision

To prepare children and youths of the community to self-reliant adults living in harmony.

Mission

In response to God's plan, Yesu Foundation exists to prepare children and youths out of poverty to become responsible adults.

Our Core Activities

Gospel Preaching

Spreading the gospel of the Lord and ensuring spiritual growth in our communities.

Fighting Poverty

Working to alleviate poverty through various community-based initiatives.

Promoting Education

Supporting children's education through sponsorships and educational resources.

Health Promotion

Providing health training, medical care, and promoting good health practices.

Better Farming

Teaching sustainable and improved farming methods to enhance food security.
