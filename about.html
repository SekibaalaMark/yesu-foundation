About YESU FOUNDATION

Learn about our history, mission, and the impact we're making in Luwero

Our Story

Following the vulnerability of communities, children and youths in the area as well as COVID effects, YESU FOUNDATION was started by Mr. Paul Zziwa and Rev. Damalie Nalubwama who are church ministers under Luwero diocese in 2021.

The foundation targets the needy of the neediest children and youths in Lukomera community and Luwero Diocese at large, aiming to transform their lives and prepare them for a better future.

Located in Luwero district, under Luwero Diocese (Anglican Faith), we work closely with local communities to identify and support those most in need.

Our Founders

Mr. Paul Zziwa and Rev. Damalie Nalubwama are dedicated church ministers with a passion for serving the most vulnerable in their community. Their vision and commitment have been the driving force behind YESU FOUNDATION's establishment and growth.

Our Vision & Mission

Vision

To prepare children and youths of the community to self-reliant adults living in harmony.

Mission

In response to God's plan, Yesu Foundation exists to prepare children and youths out of poverty to become responsible adults.

Our Objectives

We aim at producing holistic adults basing on three key aspects:

Spiritual Aspect

Ensuring that beneficiaries demonstrate their commitment to the Lordship to Christ through spiritual guidance and mentorship.

Physical Aspect

Ensuring that beneficiaries choose good health practices and maintain good physical health through training and enhancing relationships between beneficiaries, caregivers, and sponsors.

Cognitive Aspect

Ensuring that beneficiaries exhibit the motivation and skills to be economically self-supporting through empowerment and education.
