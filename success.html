Success Stories

Real stories of transformation and hope from our beneficiaries

Sekibaala Mark, 20 years old Joined in 2023

I am Sekibaala Mark, a student at Makerere University pursuing a bachelor's degree in Computer Sceince and hoping to advance with a Masters degree in Artificial intelligence and Machine Learning Engineering I joined Yesu Foundation family while I was in my form six my last year of Highschool and this family impacted me Spiritually, socially with the continued support from the heads of the family Uncle Paul Zziwa and Rev Damalie Zziwa They taught to always believe in God for everything, I had a surgery after my final exams and they prayed with me and encouraged me that I will be successful with the surgery and I was indeed successful.Am Really thankful may the good Lord enable them touch more souls

When YESU FOUNDATION identified Mark, mark thought he wouldn't make to University because some people used to doubt him but God did it, he is at the Best University in Uganda now.

"YESU FOUNDATION isn't only a family; they gave me hope and showed me that I could dream big despite my circumstances. The mentorship I received helped me believe in myself."

Today, Mark is a first-year university student pursuing a degree in Computer Sceince.He hopes to become an Artificial intelligence and Machine Learning Engineer and give back to his community by educating and helping the next generation.

Mark's love for innovation,technology and Computers keeps him going in his academics.

Above is Betty and uncle Zziwa Paul Joined in 2018

Derrick Joined 2011

Derrick's story

I'm Derick Alimani Ajuni, and I'm forever grateful for the life-changing encounter I had with Mr. Zziwa Paul in 2011 during my high school days. Our bond grew stronger in 2012 when he volunteered with Compassion Uganda and introduced me to the piano, marking the beginning of a beautiful mentorship that shaped my spiritual journey. Under his guidance, I discovered the joy of serving God and grew in my faith through attending night prayers and exploring ministry. The Zziwa family, including his loving wife Rev. Damalie Zziwa, has been a constant source of encouragement, supporting me throughout my studies and ministry endeavors. Their love and care played a significant role in my journey, enabling me to graduate as a secondary teacher and pursue community work with AVA Mission Africa. I'm now part of the Smart Band Gulu, and I'm hopeful for the future. The Zziwa family's love, kindness, and generosity have been a true blessing, and I'm grateful for their influence, praying that God continues to bless them abundantly.

"Serving the lord brings peace"

Today Derrick is a proffessional Highschool teacher and a band member who plays piano and guitar
