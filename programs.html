Our Programs

Discover how we're making a difference in the lives of children and youth

Core Activities

Gospel Preaching

We spread the gospel of the Lord through bible studies, devotions, missions, and discipleship, ensuring spiritual growth in our communities.

Fighting Poverty

We implement various initiatives to alleviate poverty in communities, including skills training, income-generating activities, and community development projects.

Promoting Education

We support children's education through school fees sponsorships, educational resources, and career guidance to help them achieve their full potential.

Health Promotion

We provide health training, medical care, and promote good health practices to ensure the physical wellbeing of our beneficiaries.

Better Farming

We teach sustainable and improved farming methods to enhance food security and provide a source of livelihood for families in our community.

Benefits for Sponsored Children

Beddings Support

We provide needy children with beddings for a better sleep and rest at home.

vocational Skills

We offer vocational skills training to the community and the beneficiaries to empower them.

Psychosocial Support

We provide counseling and emotional support to help children cope with challenges and build resilience.

Social Interactions

We organize conferences, camps, games, sports, and meetings to foster social skills and build a sense of community.

Career Guidance

We provide career counseling and guidance to help youth make informed decisions about their future.

Spiritual Growth

We facilitate exposure to the gospel of Christ through bible studies, devotions, missions, and discipleship.
